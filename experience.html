Diverse Experience

I’ve always been fascinated by languages and communication. It was a privilege to follow through on my passion and earn my Master of Education (M.Ed TESOL) from the University Of Pennsylvania Graduate School Of Education's Department of Educational Linguistics.

Even though my study at Penn was rigorous, my love for the field only kept growing. I became a Lecturer for the English Language Programs at the University of Pennsylvania, teaching international undergraduates, graduates, and working professionals who hailed from various countries. Having proved myself as a Lecturer at various institutions, I also served as a Research Assistant to the head of the department of TESOL at the University of Pennsylvania and was part of the team assigned a curriculum design project for the Abu Dhabi Education Council.

My multifaceted experience has also allowed me to work as a Help Desk agent at Cenlar FSB, an American subservicer and federally chartered service savings bank, where I was responsible for coordination and collaboration between various teams in order to develop solutions to problems. As a Help Desk agent, I was also tasked with developing solutions to the various technical challenges that presented themselves during the Citibank portfolio transfer and acquisition of Citibank employees.

At work, my diligence and attention to detail have always been recognized – serving as the number one reason why I was always tasked with the responsibility of writing training materials and articles for the internal Footprints Knowledge Base. Leveraging my exceptional communication skills, I conduct techno-functional liaison to understand the requirements of each project or paper thoroughly before I start developing pertinent and professional solutions.

Recognizing the importance of technological knowledge and technical skills, I am currently enrolled in a part time online Fullstack Software Development diploma course at Edinburgh Napier University. Throughout this course, I am learning front end and backend technologies including JavaScript, MongoDB, JQuery, JSON, and Python. Payment processing is also an important part of this course which will add to the experience that I had gained throughout my time at Cenlar.

My love for the world of technology is also validated by technological certifications which include Linux Essentials, CompTIA Network+, CompTIA Security+, and CompTIA A+. I am also a certified Amazon Web Services Cloud Practitioner, IT Operations Specialist, AWS Certified Solutions Architect Associate, and a Secure Infrastructure Specialist.
